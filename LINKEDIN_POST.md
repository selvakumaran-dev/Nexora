## 💡 POSTING TIPS

1. **Best times to post on LinkedIn:**
   - Tuesday - Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12 PM
   - Avoid weekends and Monday mornings

2. **Engagement boosters:**
   - Ask a question at the end
   - Use emojis sparingly but strategically
   - Include 3-5 relevant hashtags (not too many!)
   - Respond to comments within the first hour

3. **First sentence matters:**
   - Make it attention-grabbing
   - Avoid starting with "I'm excited to announce..."
   - Try hooks like: "After 6 months of building in silence..."

4. **Call to action:**
   - "What do you think?"
   - "Would you use something like this?"
   - "Drop your thoughts below!"
